A small experimentals project to explore ray marching. The rendering was done in Unity URP, with a custom shader built using a mix of HLSL and Shader Graph. The lamp model was created and textured in Blender. It’s inspired by a vintage lava lamp from my childhood that still sits on a nearby shelf. The digital version looks a bit cleaner though, since the real lamp’s liquid has aged in far less appealing ways.

Unity Render. The lava does not account for volume conservation, the bottom and top parts do not grow or shrink based on how much wax is added or removed. It could be faked by carefully positioning the blobs that go up and down in the right place.
